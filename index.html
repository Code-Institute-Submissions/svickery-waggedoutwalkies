Services & Prices

Dog Walking

Our dog walking service insures your pooch will get the essential exercise your pet needs. We will pick up your dog from your home and take them for a walk service of your choosing. When we return your dog, we make sure there is water available and that they are toweled off if wet.

  • 30 Min Walk (Solo) - £10
  • 60 Min Walk (Solo) - £12
  • 60 Min Walk (Group) - £11
Puppy Care

We know how important the early stages are for your pup and how essential it is that they are stimulated and cared for. We offer a service to spend time with your new puppy and make sure they are toileted, watered, played with and fussed they settle in to their new home. You'll have peace of mind while you take care of all your life admin!

  • 30 Min Visit - £10
  • 60 Min Visit - £11
  • 90 Min Visit - £12
Dog Sitting

If you're going away for a few days or a nice holiday, we will take care of your dog in your own home. This way your pooch will be less stressed and happier when you return. We will feed and walk your dog twice per day and ensure they are loved and fussed, enhancing your relaxtion while away.

  • 24 Hours £40
  • 1 Week £250
  • Additional Dog £10 (Per Day)
